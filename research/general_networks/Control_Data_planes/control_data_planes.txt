Control, Data/Forwarding planes

Control Plane
> Functions are system configuration, management and exchange of routing
table information
> Routers exchange information with other routers and builds a routing
table based on the routing protocol
> Drawing network topology
> Decides where traffic is sent

Data Plane/Forwarding plane
> The 2 terms data/forwarding seem to be interchangeable
> Forwards the data to the next hop in the path according to control plane
logic
